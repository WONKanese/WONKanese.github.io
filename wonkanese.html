General Overview Of North Korean Willy Wonka Society


The most powerful being that is controlling the universe is North Korean Willy Wonka (NKWW). NKWW created every planet, therefore created humans. We think humanity is the first species to discover NKWW, humanity discovered NKWW thanks to Gandi, the controller of lighting and finder of Wonkanese. Gandi discovered Wonkanese when he was struck by lightning 173,145,158 times in the span of 10 years. He almost died after the first three strikes, but he believed he was absorbing them and suprisingly, he gained power each time he got hit. After the 10 years, he was so powerful he ascended to the skies and met the former controller of the sky; Zeus. They fought in a gruesome battle but gandi was too powerful. After gandi won his mind was flooded with every Wonkanese knowledge there is. Gandi was a human and therefore spreaded this information to all of humanity, he told them to sacrifice oompa loompa salads (the worst beings in the universe) to NKWW. To find oompa loompa salads, people volunteer to be oompa loompas to sacrifice half of their eyesight to NKWW so NKWW can find oompa loompa salads.
