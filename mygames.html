Tall Tower


Tall Tower was the first game I made with Unity. Tall tower is a very simple concept, using only the left and right arrow keys and your unusual body, you try to climb to the top of a tower. This game might seem easy, but it's fucking impossible; There is no checkpoints and you can lose huge progress by one misclick.

Final Hope


Final hope is my biggest project by far. I started making this game in late april of 2022. In Final Hope you are in a toxic wasteland that is earth in the future, Earth is overrun with 2 major viruses destroying the earth, your goal is to survive and thrive in this fantasty world. Final hope is clearly inspired by Terraria, although it is much different in many ways. You might think this game is hard, and if you do, your correct, this game is even harder than tall tower. In fact, it's going to be the hardest game you've ever played.
